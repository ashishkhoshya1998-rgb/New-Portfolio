## Overview

*Detailed content coming soon — this section will cover the TimePay vertical of the Zzazz.ai ecosystem.*

TimePay became the linchpin of the entire ecosystem. Per-minute content access — the micro-transaction layer. Users pay only for what they consume, removing the commitment barrier of full-price purchases.

## The Key Insight

Users don't resist paying — they resist committing to an uncertain outcome. A $5 article feels risky. But $0.02 per minute feels like nothing — and after 10 minutes, you've paid $0.20 and received clear value.

## Three-Path Access Model

<ProcessStep number="01" title="Full Access">
  One-time purchase at the creator's set price. For users who know what they want and trust the creator. Highest margin, lowest volume.
</ProcessStep>

<ProcessStep number="02" title="TimePay">
  Per-minute micro-payments. For discovery and sampling. Lowest barrier, highest conversion to engagement. Micro-transaction friction decreases by 73% when users can see real-time cost accumulation.
</ProcessStep>

<ProcessStep number="03" title="Exchange Credit">
  Users earn credits through engagement, sharing, and contributing — then spend credits on content. Creates a circular economy where participation generates purchasing power.
</ProcessStep>

## Impact

> "1.9% subscription penetration. 57% leave at paywalls. Cash-per-article fails in India's market. The three-path model was designed to capture the 80–98% who'd never subscribe."
