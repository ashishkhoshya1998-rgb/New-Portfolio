Pricing content is a market design problem, not a UX problem. Solving it took seven verticals — each addressing a different gap in adoption, monetisation, or trust. Removing any one of them breaks the system.

Each card below is a vertical I architected. The deep-dive case studies are still being written — they'll cover the specific design problem each one solves, the decisions behind its interaction model, and how it plugs back into the larger ecosystem.
