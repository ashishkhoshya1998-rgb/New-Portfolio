## Overview

Signal Pill is the external-facing layer of ZZAZZ — a pricing widget embedded on partner publisher websites that surfaces the ecosystem where users already read. It existed as a basic integration layer when I joined. I reframed its role in the ecosystem and redesigned the experience.

**My contribution: Rethink & redesign** — reframed Signal Pill from a simple pricing display into a strategic entry point for the ecosystem.

## The Reframe

The original Signal Pill showed a price next to an article on a partner publisher's website. That's useful — but it's passive. It displays information without creating a pathway into the ecosystem.

I reframed Signal Pill's role: it's not just a price tag. It's the **first touchpoint** where most users encounter ZZAZZ. For users who never visit the Terminal or install the Plugin, Signal Pill might be the only surface they interact with. It needs to do more than display a number.

## What Signal Pill Communicates

<ProcessStep number="01" title="Content Value Signal">
  The LPM-generated price appears alongside the article, giving readers an independent quality signal. Higher-priced content is perceived as more trustworthy — validated in user research.
</ProcessStep>

<ProcessStep number="02" title="Market Context">
  How this piece of content compares to similar articles in the market. Is it priced above or below average? What does that signal about its quality or timeliness?
</ProcessStep>

<ProcessStep number="03" title="Access Pathway">
  For users who want to engage deeper — access premium content, see detailed pricing, or explore related content — Signal Pill provides a bridge into the full ZZAZZ ecosystem.
</ProcessStep>

## Strategic Role

Signal Pill is the **Widget form factor** of ZZAZZ's distribution strategy. It sits at the beginning of the graduation path:

**Signal Pill** (discover pricing on publisher sites) → **Plugin** (explore across the web) → **Terminal** (commit to the full ecosystem)

For publishers, Signal Pill adds value without disrupting their existing reading experience. For ZZAZZ, it creates awareness and adoption at the largest possible scale — embedded in the reading experiences users already have.

## Design Principles

- **Non-intrusive** — enhances the reading experience, never disrupts it
- **Contextually relevant** — the pricing information adds meaning to what the user is already reading
- **Gateway, not destination** — provides just enough value to spark curiosity about the broader ecosystem
- **Publisher-friendly** — respects the publisher's design language and reading experience
