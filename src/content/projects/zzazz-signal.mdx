Signal is the intelligence layer — aggregated signals from user behavior, pricing trends, and content performance that feed back into the ecosystem to optimize discovery and pricing.

## Role in the Ecosystem

Signal connects all other verticals by collecting and distributing intelligence. What users search for in Exchange, how they consume via TimePay, what creators publish — all of these generate signals that make the entire ecosystem smarter over time.
