## Overview

*Detailed content coming soon — this section will cover the Moments vertical of the Zzazz.ai ecosystem.*

Moments is a new vertical I created from scratch. Time-sensitive, short-form content with dynamic pricing — creating genuine urgency through temporal scarcity. Content that exists for a window, priced by its freshness.

## The Problem

No creator monetization existed from day one for short-form content. Existing platforms reward follower count over content quality, making it impossible for new creators to monetize.

## Design Approach

I set the base, defined full IA + wireframes, and delegated UI to a team member. The core principle: **content quality > follower count** for monetization.
