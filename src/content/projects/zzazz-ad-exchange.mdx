## Overview

The Ad Exchange is the advertising layer — connecting brands with priced content environments. Advertisers pay premium rates because priced content signals engaged, intentional audiences.

## The Problem

Cookie targeting is dying. Platforms need a new model for connecting advertisers with relevant audiences without invasive tracking.

## Design Approach

I designed content-intent contextual ads — a cricket article about Kohli's century shows cricket equipment ads. The content itself becomes the targeting signal, not user tracking data.
