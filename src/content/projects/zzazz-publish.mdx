## Overview

*Detailed content coming soon — this section will cover the Publish vertical of the Zzazz.ai ecosystem.*

Publish is the creator studio — tools for publishing priced content with built-in distribution. Designed to make the act of pricing content as natural as the act of writing it.

## The Problem

Publish existed with basic metrics only. Publishers had no way to understand how their content was performing in the pricing ecosystem or make informed decisions about content strategy.

## Design Approach

### Universal vs Modular

Publishers operate differently. I chose a **modular per workflow** approach — modular components with consistent pricing logic underneath. Each publisher's dashboard reflects their workflow, not a one-size-fits-all template.

## Key Outcome

Publishers adopted pricing internally before showing users — validating standalone value of the pricing tools.
