## Overview

*Detailed content coming soon — this section will cover the DOTS Protocol vertical of the Zzazz.ai ecosystem.*

DOTS is the trust and reputation system — creator credibility scores that make pricing transparent. Users can see *why* content is priced the way it is based on creator track record.

## The Problem

Copying breaks revenue — 50K bot scrapes/day vs 20 humans reading. Without provenance tracking, creators lose control and revenue the moment their content is shared or repurposed.

## Design Approach

Cryptographic lineage where derivatives link to originals with automatic revenue splits. Creators participate in the downstream value chain — even when their content is remixed, quoted, or built upon.
